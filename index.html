E-SUMMIT

The Entrepreneurship Summit is the flagship
event of E-Cell which is a platform for bringing
together budding entrepreneurs, venture
capitalists, investors, start-ups and other
major contributors to the entrepreneurial
ecosystem.

About US

Entrepreneurship is the process of designing,
launching and running a new business, which is
often initially a small business. The people who
create these businesses are
called entrepreneurs. And with the growing
India, the numbers of young aspirants who want
to start their own venture are growing rapidly.

This networking event gives a chance for
people to interact and help build successful
ventures.

The summit also showcases the latest global
trends in entrepreneurship.

The million pitch

Present your ideas,
products or services to the renowned panel of
investors and a chance to win a million (INR) for the
swift transformation of your start-up.

CASCADE MEETUP

Get an opportunity to receive a 360-degree feedback from various people belonging to different backgrounds. Showcase your start-up to investors, customers, and public. 

INFLUENCING 

A talk by the speakers, investors, entrepreneurs, and industrialists to explore the possibilities of the new horizons of the markets, combining research and entrepreneurship.
